USS Nightingale

Summary

Location:: The Shackleton Expanse
Year:: 2382
Portrait::
The NCC-60805-C: A colony support and emergency response ship.

Description

A Federation Intrepid Class starship commanded by Captain Robin Zhao. It has a mission profile for colony support and is a first responder ship for emergencies.

Traits

  • Federation Ship
  • Intrepid Class
  • Hope Ship

Namesake

The USS Nightingale is the 3rd ship of its name.

  • The original Nightingale was a small medical supply ship in the 2280s.
  • In the in the 2370s, the NCC-60805-B was a Nebula Class hospital ship commanded by Captain Nandi Jadeja. The 60805-B was sent behind enemy lines during the Cardassian insurgency, where it collected escape pods and other survivors.
  • The current NCC-60805-C Nightingale was commissioned in 2373 and has been serving as a colony support vessel for nearly 10 years

Mission

The Nightingale is outfitted for colony support and provides cargo transfers, medical backup, and miliary presence to new colony worlds. These colonies are the kind of places that deal with the unknowns which still appear even after years of survey and study, so the Nightingale is equipped to response flexibly to a wide variety of needs.

Mission Logs

Season 1
  • Nightingale Log 0:
    Stardate -
    The crew of the USS Nightingale is recruited and prepared for duty (and friendship!)
  • Nightingale Log 1.1 - Bacchus-3:
    Stardate 56241
    The Nightingale arrived at Bacchus 3, where they discovered some colonists trying to activate some ancient alien ruins. The planet itself had a warp core signature at its center.
  • Nightingale Log 1.2 - Bacchus-3:
    Stardate 56452
    The colonists of Bacchus-3 were found to have their organic material resonating with the subspace distortion waves and their mass phased into subspace. The Klingons joined the Nightingale while the away team has entered the monolith, only to trigger it’s systems.
  • Nightingale Log 1.3 - Bacchus-3:
    Stardate 56452
    The Nightingale successfully evacuated the remaining colonists from Bacchus-3 and recovered an active buffer of other lifeforms, including what we believe are the remaining missing colonists. In the process, we deactivated the alien device causing the subspace distortion waves and rescued a Klingon ship from a warp core containment breach. The Nightingale is towing the Klingon ship back to Narendra station for repairs and to receive our next assignment.
  • Nightingale Log 2 A Klingon Christmas:
    Stardate 56980.82
    The Nightingale was assigned on a diplomatic mission to deliver toys of violence to children on the Klingon-Federation border. The crew was assaulted by the children, but creatively managed to deliver the gifts with minimal harm to crew or the children. The final stop involved creating a fireworks show by blowing up a shuttle. In this incident, Lieutenant Velara nearly died but was beamed off the exploding shuffle at the last moment and saved by the medical staff.
  • Nightingale Log 3.1 - Orion Heist:
    Stardate 57005
    A few members of the Nightingale were recruited by Admiral Stoker to find and retrieve a stolen artifact similar to the monument found on Bacchus-III. The away team took a shuttle, the USS Mackenzie, to chase after the SS Castle Rock, an Orion merchant freighter. There the Castle Rock suffered a breach when the back end crumpled from subspace distortion waves.
  • Nightingale Log 3.2 - Orion Heist:
    Stardate 57019
    The Crew of the USS Mackenzie boarded the Orion Castle Rock to retrieve the stolen artifact. One of the Orions was cybernetically connected with the artifact using a neural net and seemingly hypnotized by it. After a brief combat encounter, the artifact was retrieved. The Orions agreed to take a well made counterfeit copy of the artifact to sell to the Romulans.
  • Nightingale Log 4 - Peas to Meet You:
    Stardate 57030
    The crew assisted the colony of Re Alpha to transport it's bountiful produce to the struggling colony at the Yacci system. However, a popular treat called Ice Peas had an unexpected reaction to being transplanted. The crew all experienced enhanced attraction to one another until the medical and science teams were able to develop a cure.
  • Nightingale Log 5.1 - Simplicity:
    Stardate 56748
    Admiral Banna Stoker boarded the Nightingale and took command of the ship. After arriving at Pasqual-I, an away team was beamed down to investigate the electromagnetic field surrounding the planet's surface. Meanwhile, another Federation vessel arrived to retrieve the admiral, by orders from Starfleet.
  • Nightingale Log 5.2 - Complexity:
    Stardate 56748
    The Nightingale's away team investigated the Tilikaal obelisk on Pasqual-1 and discovered that it was forcing an accelerated evolution for life on the planet. It was attempting to make contact with the Tilikaal but had been abandoned for thousands of years. The away team deactivated the obelisk but then were beamed off the planet by the USS Walcott. The Walcott had confronted the Nightingale to demand custody of Admiral Stoker. However, the Nightingale fled into warp speed rather than fight the Walcott.
  • Nightingale Log 5.3 - Clarity:
    Stardate 56748
    The Nightingale defended itself against a Cardassian battlecruiser, apparently with a bounty on Admiral Stoker. After the battlecruiser was defeated, Captain robin Zhao received a transmission from the USS Walcott informing them that Admiral Stoker was likely compromised by Tilikaal influence. Stoker was arrested. Her companion, Kassandra Pruitt and the alien entity possessing her, was interviewed aboard the Nightingale. Ultimately, the entity was expelled from Pruitt using a subspace anti-resonance.
  • Nightingale Log 6 - Ghostbusters:
    Stardate 56748
    The Tilikaal entity was released from Kassandra Pruitt, and quickly fled into the rest of the USS Nightingale. The crews of the Nightingale and Walcott cooperated to find it. However, the entity was able to possess various other officers to get control of the Tilikaal Artifact. Along with several other Tilikaal entities, apparently gaining access to real space through the artifact, it attempted to pull the entire ship into subspace, but was thwarted by a heroic sacrifice by Lt. Grey.
  • Nightingale Log 7 - Border Dispute:
    Stardate 56906
    The Nightingale was summoned to rescue the USS Hudson, a Nova class science ship that had unexpectedly veered into Romulan space. A Romulan Warbird fired upon and disabled the Hudson. A rescue team was sent to the Hudson to investigate and provide help. There. they discovered a Romulan spy posing as a Vulcan. As the warbird wrestled wit the Hudson using a tractor beam, both sides began beaming crew off the ship. Siltec attempted to stop the spy from escaping on the Nightingale, unaware that the Romulans were beaming the spy off. Siltec has not been seen since.
  • Nightingale Log 8 - Order in Court:
    Stardate 56942
    While the crew of the USS Nightingale was on shore leave, Admiral Stoker stood trial, with Captain Zhao, Ensign Kader, and Lt. Relza among the witnesses. Prosecutor Loxor argued Stoker endangered Starfleet, while her lawyer Tokmoth questioned whether Stoker truly intended harm. It was found that Stoker's behavior could have been caused by ideas incepted into Stoker's mind.
  • Nightingale Log 9.1 - Rescue:
    Stardate 57017
    At a technology convention arranged to lure out Romulan agents, Siltec was forced to identify Cardassian engineers under threat to his mother, Misham Nol. Ensign Algan telepathically recognized Siltec, but a Starfleet officer was assassinated as a warning, plunging the convention into chaos. Misham was safely beamed away as Romulan agents opened fire.
  • Nightingale Log 9.2 - Home Safe:
    Stardate 57026
    After Misham Nol’s evacuation, Romulan agents were arrested while their delegation beamed out. Nol lashed out at Robin Zhao and Ewasi, blaming them for Siltec’s endangerment and trying to convince her son to leave Starfleet, but Siltec refused. Later, the crew attended a promotion ceremony at Narendra Station where Algan Kader was promoted, Zephyr Grey was remembered, and Robin bid farewell to Yalis and Relza before their transfer.
Season 2
  • Nightingale Log 10.1 - Looking into the Abyss:
    Stardate 57056, Session: 16
    The Nightingale pursued Romulans to Omega Draconis, where a black whole seemed to suddenly threaten life on an orbiting planet and also systems up to 100 light years away. The people living on the orbiting planet awaited their ascension, believing they were in no danger. Meanwhile, an away team entered a Tilikaal space station to find the Romulans in the control room.
  • Nightingale Log 10.2 - Abyss Station:
    Stardate 58191, Session: 17
    The away team on Abyss Station defeated the Romulans in the control room. Rala disabled the security systems, and Siltec helped sway the Romulans to defect. The crew began efforts to realign the black hole and destroy the station. Holly Jones disappeared during the mission, raising concerns.
  • Nightingale Log 10.3 - Displacement:
    Stardate 59240, Session: 18
    Atsad confirmed Akkara was 4,000 years out of time and infected with Tilikaal nanites. The team stabilized the black hole and sabotaged the station's singularity engines, trigginging a collapse of the entire solar system. The Ithik were beamed off their planet without full consent.
  • Nightingale Log 10 - Finding Faith:
    Stardate 59279, Session: 19
    Tensions with the evacuated Ithik escalated as Mayor Akkara accused Starfleet of violating their faith. Akkara's clone, avoided contact with his people, but was eventually persuaded to reach out to them, causing division amongst them. The crew eased tension by constructing a religious space for them aboard the ship, and started planning an asteroid relocation to construct a new home.
  • Nightingale Log 11.1 Crystal Clear:
    Stardate 59317, Session: 20
    The Nightingale investigated a mysterious crystalline vessel near Narendra Station, discovering hundreds of non-organic sentiences within and a lodged titanium torpedo. Attempts to communicate failed as the ship began discharging energy, and Siltec and Rala pushed through heavy fire to secure the torpedo. When it was transported out, it exploded, leaving both officers critically injured and breaching the vessel’s hull.
  • Nightingale Log 11.2 - Shattered Relationships:
    Stardate 59317, Session: 21
    The torpedo on the cystal ship exploded, injuring Siltec and Rala with shrapnel. The Crystal ship split apart and in the chaos, the Zephyr industry freighters took off with stolen fragments. The crew returned to the Nightingale, tended to injuries, and debriefed. Several officers shared the blame. The Nightingale tracked down the Zephyr freighters to the Pasqual system. Kader and Terzin remained behind to help the injured alien entities, and The Nightingale prepared to follow.
  • Nightingale Log 12.1 - Pasqual's Progress:
    Stardate 59317, Session: 22
    The Nightingale crew followed Zephyr Industry freighters to the Pasqual system, where the civilian corporation had lead the removal of Tilikaal artifacts from Pasqual-1. There, locals influenced by visions of an "angel" were using radio signals to call for an alien encounter. An away team discovered the Pasqual locals excavating a facility housing cybernetically enhanced humanoids similar to the Ithik.
  • Nightingale Log 12.2 - Assimilation:
    Stardate 59317, Session: 23
    The crew discovered a crashed Borg vessel beneath Pasqual-1, where dormant drones and a preserved cybernetic head called the “Face of Borg” had recently reactivated. The Borg believed the Tilikaal to be their creators and sought to assimilate them to achieve perfection. Rather than destroy them, the Nightingale crew sedated the locals, disabled the vessel’s communications, and placed the Borg head into containment for study.
  • Nightingale Log 12.3 - Liberation:
    Stardate 59317, Session: 24
    The Ujul and Rala developed nanites to disinfect the Ithik-Borg on Pasqual-1. As soon as it gained independance, Rala saw in its memories that it was purposely cloned to be assimilated. When the villagers nearby woke up from the sleeping gas, Vrenta calmed calmed them down and began teaching them about the path to space travel. Holly shuttled the “Face of Borg” into hyper-secure containment aboard the Nightingale.
