USS Nightingale

Summary

Location:: The Shackleton Expanse
Year:: 2381
Portrait::
The NCC-60805-C: A colony support and emergency response ship.

Description

A Federation Intrepid Class starship commanded by Captain Robin Zhao. It has a mission profile for colony support and is a first responder ship for emergencies.

Traits

  • Federation Ship
  • Intrepid Class
  • Hope Ship

Namesake

The USS Nightingale is the 3rd ship of its name.

  • The original Nightingale was a small medical supply ship in the 2280s.
  • In the in the 2370s, the NCC-60805-B was a Nebula Class hospital ship commanded by Captain Nandi Jadeja. The 60805-B was sent behind enemy lines during the Cardassian insurgency, where it collected escape pods and other survivors.
  • The current NCC-60805-C Nightingale was commissioned in 2373 and has been serving as a colony support vessel for nearly 10 years

Mission

The Nightingale is outfitted for colony support and provides cargo transfers, medical backup, and miliary presence to new colony worlds. These colonies are the kind of places that deal with the unknowns which still appear even after years of survey and study, so the Nightingale is equipped to response flexibly to a wide variety of needs.

    Stardate 57020.6
    Starfleet Intelligence sources believed Siltec to be in custody of the Romulans as they attempted to learn more about the Cardassian Disruptor Spinal Lance technology. To lure his captors out, they organized a science and technology convention in the name of peace and cooperation across the Alpha and Beta quadrants. The crew of the Nightingale was brought in to display their findings from research in the Shackleton Expanse.

    During the convention, Siltec was disguised as a Romulan and told to identify Cardassian engineers who worked on the Spinal Lance. Though reluctant to comply, Siltec's mother, Misham Nol, was conspicuously present at the convention. Siltec's handler, Sub-Commander Thal'vaek threatened to kill her if he did not cooperate.

    Ensign Algan quickly identified Siltec telepathically, and notified the crew and Starfleet Intelligence. But when a Starfleet officer was killed near Misham as a warning, the innovation floor exploded into chaos. Misham was beamed out of the convention by Starfleet, just as several other Romulan agents fired on her.

    After the evacuation of Misham Nol, the Romulan agents responsible were quickly stunned and arrested. The Romulan delegation themselves meanwhile set up pattern enhancers and beamed out of the convention center.

    Misham Nol was unhappy with Starfleet handling of Siltec's situation, and blamed both Robin Zhao and Ewasi personally for the danger Siltec was in. Siltec disarmed her with loving understanding. Starfleet needs him and it's where he belongs.

The Orions agreed to take a well made counterfeit copy of the artifact to sell to the Romulans.

However, a popular treat called Ice Peas had an unexpected reaction to being transplanted. The crew all experienced enhanced attraction to one another until the medical and science teams were able to develop a cure.
  • Nightingale Log 5.1 - Simplicity:
    Stardate 56748
    Admiral Banna Stoker boarded the Nightingale and took command of the ship. After arriving at Pasqual-I, an away team was beamed down to investigate the electromagnetic field surrounding the planet's surface. Meanwhile, another Federation vessel arrived to retrieve the admiral, by orders from Starfleet.
  • Nightingale Log 5.2 - Complexity:
    Stardate 56748
    The Nightingale's away team investigated the Tilikaal obelisk on Pasqual-1 and discovered that it was forcing an accelerated evolution for life on the planet. It was attempting to make contact with the Tilikaal but had been abandoned for thousands of years. The away team deactivated the obelisk but then were beamed off the planet by the USS Walcott. The Walcott had confronted the Nightingale to demand custody of Admiral Stoker. However, the Nightingale fled into warp speed rather than fight the Walcott.
  • Nightingale Log 5.3 - Clarity:
    Stardate 56748
    The Nightingale defended itself against a Cardassian battlecruiser, apparently with a bounty on Admiral Stoker. After the battlecruiser was defeated, Captain robin Zhao received a transmission from the USS Walcott informing them that Admiral Stoker was likely compromised by Tilikaal influence. Stoker was arrested. Her companion, Kassandra Pruitt and the alien entity possessing her, was interviewed aboard the Nightingale. Ultimately, the entity was expelled from Pruitt using a subspace anti-resonance.
  • Nightingale Log 7 - Ghostbusters:
    Stardate 56748
    The Tilikaal entity was released from Kassandra Pruitt, and quickly fled into the rest of the USS Nightingale. The crews of the Nightingale and Walcott cooperated to find it. However, the entity was able to possess various other officers to get control of the Tilikaal Artifact. Along with several other Tilikaal entities, apparently gaining access to real space through the artifact, it attempted to pull the entire ship into subspace, but was thwarted by a heroic sacrifice by Lt. Grey.
  • Nightingale Log 8 - Border Dispute:
    Stardate 56906
    The Nightingale was summoned to rescue the USS Hudson, a Nova class science ship that had unexpectedly veered into Romulan space. A Romulan Warbird fired upon and disabled the Hudson. A rescue team was sent to the Hudson to investigate and provide help. There. they discovered a Romulan spy posing as a Vulcan. As the warbird wrestled wit the Hudson using a tractor beam, both sides began beaming crew off the ship. Siltec attempted to stop the spy from escaping on the Nightingale, unaware that the Romulans were beaming the spy off. Siltec has not been seen since.
  • Nightingale Log 9 - Order in Court:
    Stardate 56942
    While the crew of the USS Nightingale was on shore leave, Admiral Stoker stood trial, with Captain Zhao, Ensign Kader, and Lt. Relza among the witnesses. Prosecutor Loxor argued Stoker endangered Starfleet, while her lawyer Tokmoth questioned whether Stoker truly intended harm. It was found that Stoker's behavior could have been caused by ideas incepted into Stoker's mind.

    Ensign Kader telepathically heard the judge's planning to convict Stoker unfairly in order to cover up Starfleet Intelligence secrets. After exposing this with help from Tokmoth and Yalis, the judge was arrested and a mistrial declared. Stoker resigned her commission shortly after the trial.
Season 2
  • Nightingale Log 11 - Looking into the Abyss:
    Stardate 57101
    The Nightingale pursued Romulans to Omega Draconis, where a black whole seemed to suddenly threaten life on an orbiting planet and also systems up to 100 light years away. The people living on the orbiting planet awaited their ascension, believing they were in no danger. Meanwhile, an away team entered a Tilikaal space station to find the Romulans in the control room.
    The Nightingale was sent on a mission to the Omega Draconis system to find out what the Romulans were doing there. There, a single planet and a Neutronium space station orbiting a black hole. The black hole itself began rotating, so that it's radiation jets were aligned with the galactic plane. This threatened to utterly destroy the planet, but also throw radiation up to 100 light years away.

    On the planet, a landing party found a small colony of less than 1000 people called the Ithik worshiping the black hole. They seemed excited to finally have the Abyss gaze back at them, after they had observed it for thousands of years. The landing party made some attempts to explain that the black hole would obliterate them, but the Ithik remained convinced they would ascend to a high form of existence when the truth of the universe is revealed to them through the black hole.

    The Ithik had with them a single artifact from the Tilikaal, similar to the artifact that Banna Stoker used. Commander Vrenta used the artifact to recieve a vision of the Neutronium space station. The landing party then took the artifact, beamed onto a shuttle, and went straight to the space station with only 2 hours until Chaktir would be destroyed.

    Siltec used the artifact as a key to enter the station. Inside, they found a small Romulan scout ship in a docking bay. Siltec was able to pick up trace heat signatures left over from when the Romulans passed by. The away team split up into two teams. Siltec and Vrenta found large security robots lined with Neutronium plugged into stations along the walls. Holly Jones and Ujul Atsad found a cloning facility and released a person who claimed to be Akkara, the "mayor" of Chaktir. They were confused about how they got there but remembered being visited by Tilikaal on Chaktir.

    Vrenta and Siltec were able to finally locate the station's control room, and sent directions to Cmdr. Jones and Lt. Asad. But the Romulans were already inside.
