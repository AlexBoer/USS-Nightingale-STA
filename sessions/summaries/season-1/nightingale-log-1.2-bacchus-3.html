Yalis
The colonists, Gray, and Velara went up to the Nightingale on the shuttle while Siltec, Janus and I stayed on the planet. I was able to interpret some of the runes on the monolith and discovered one of them identified a button for opening the monolith.

A Klingon shuttle was approaching, but turned back when Janus and I informed them the planet was under quarantine. The colonists on the ship were also placed in quarantine by the EMH.

While we were dealing with that, Siltec went to look for the rest of the colonists. He didn't find them, but he took his shoes off for some reason and contact with the planet made his feet get glowy/blue and start phasing into subspace. (We also found out that colonists who were blue had stopped showing up/disappeared, and were concerned this would happen to Siltec's feet.)

I used the colonists' dish to transmit an inverse wave at Siltec's feet and restore them to normal space. When the team on the ship returned, I asked them to bring a metal tape measure, and built a yagi that can act as a portable de-bluer. I also informed the EMH and Chief Sa'mi of the solution I had discovered.

The Klingon shuttle went to the Nightingale, where Gray and Velara talked to them. They did a very good job convincing the Klingons to cooperate with our handling of the situation, and earned the respect of the Klingons (when Commander Gluk was rude, Velara said she didn't know they let targs command ships). They didn't seem too concerned about D'Loq and Brutog (the two Klingon colonists in sickbay), they were just upset that their ship had been knocked out of warp.

When Gray and Velara came back to the surface, we opened the monolith. Janus was under the influence of the monolith and tried to run inside, but Siltec stopped her and I de-blued her. Her medical tricorder was thrown in and hit by a defensive beam. Siltec knocked out the security sensors and we continued inside.

When we got to the control room, the computer verbally greeted us and recognized us as a worthy species. It then began an activation sequence. Holograms depicted the monoliths drawing mass from nearby planets, which is probably what they're being activated to do.
Yalis Log 1.2

Robin Zhao
We've made marginal progress towards escaping from Bacchus 3. The engineering team is still unable to bring us to warp through the subspace distortion waves.

Several colonists have been recovered. Each of the colonists seem to have their organic material brought into resonance with the subspace waves. The more they resonate with the waves, the further they fall into subspace. Yalis has counteracted the resonance with a differential subspace signal to cancel out the noise on a local level.

Several members of the Klingon starship Ya'Leth have also come aboard and are speaking with our medical and engineering teams. They have been unable to bring their warp core online. I've offered to send over an engineering team as soon as possible.
