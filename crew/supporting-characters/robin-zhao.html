Robin Zhao Log 1.1Stardate 56241 - Nightingale Log 1.1 - Bacchus-3
We've arrived at the Bacchus system after a power failure that nearly through the Nightingale into the system's star. The source of the failure seems to be a massive subspace disruption from ancient ruins on the planet.

I have sent an away team to investigate the surface. Meanwhile a Klingon Bird of Prey fell out of warp with a power failure similar to what we experiences. We have caught the Klingon ship in our tractor beam until they are able to restore power to their systems.

Both communications and warp travel seem difficulty or even impossible unless we can deactivate the source of the subspace distortion waves.
Robin Zhao Log 1.2Stardate 56452 - Nightingale Log 1.2 - Bacchus-3
We've made marginal progress towards escaping from Bacchus 3. The engineering team is still unable to bring us to warp through the subspace distortion waves.

Several colonists have been recovered. Each of the colonists seem to have their organic material brought into resonance with the subspace waves. The more they resonate with the waves, the further they fall into subspace. Yalis has counteracted the resonance with a differential subspace signal to cancel out the noise on a local level.

Several members of the Klingon starship Ya'Leth have also come aboard and are speaking with our medical and engineering teams. They have been unable to bring their warp core online. I've offered to send over an engineering team as soon as possible.

For now, I'm awaiting a report from the surface team about.... hold on. Captain Zhao to the bridge: Is that a tractor beam coming out of that planet?

Captain Zhao out.
