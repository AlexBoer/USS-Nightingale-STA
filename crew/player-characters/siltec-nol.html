Note:: Formerly recruited by Starfleet Intelligence. Now just looking to do some good in the world.

Siltec was born on Earth after his mother, a Legate in the Obsidian Order, showed up at Jupiter Station with 1000 strips of gold pressed latinum and a stolen Cardassian shuttle. She exchanged information for certain assurances, including the protection of her unborn son.

Starfleet agreed and set them up in a safehouse: Zefram Cochrane's old Montana ranch, which had since been converted into a cultural center and basic survival training post for Starfleet cadets. Siltec grew up with a love for horses and big open night skies. He would lay on the roof at night and count the stars he would visit when he got to join Starfleet.

His mother did not approve, and used her influence to ensure his bid for the academy would not be accepted. But she could stop him from enlisting. She could, however, influence where his first assignment was. Siltec's heart dropped when he opened his duty papers and saw that he was headed right back to the ranch where he grew up, under the watchful eye of his mother.

He spent a few years running young Starfleet officers through survival training bootcamp, then watched them leave to follow his dream among the stars. When Starfleet Intelligence dropped by to offer him a deal, he leapt at the chance.

Starfleet gets two things from this deal:

  • Siltec is a good agent.
  • Once his mom got him back in the nest, she wasn't nearly as forthcoming with information, so Starfleet sees this as a way to use her son's safety (and constant updates on his whereabouts and actions) as a negotiating tool.

Siltec only had one request: a billet aboard the USS Nightingale and a path to adventure.
